This lab is the canonical sandbox for the rebuilt stickman runtime. It is where I validate clip blending, body facing, and attachment behavior before those assets are reused in project-facing motion pieces.

The current prop pass includes a small VR headset family so I can compare reads without changing the underlying rig:

- `VR Headset`: the original solid visor block
- `VR Wire Dark`: black edge with white fill
- `VR Wire Light`: white edge with black fill
- `Boba Headset`: a rounded front silhouette with a centered nose cut derived from the Boba project direction
